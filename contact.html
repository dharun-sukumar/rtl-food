Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is 25-35 minutes. During peak hours (lunch and dinner), it may take up to 45 minutes. We'll provide you with real-time tracking once your order is confirmed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and major food delivery app payments. Payment is secure and encrypted.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver within a 15-mile radius of our main restaurant. Delivery fee varies from $2.99-$4.99 based on distance. Free delivery on orders over $35!

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes! You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, please call us immediately at (555) 123-FOOD and we'll try our best to accommodate your request.

Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?

Absolutely! We have a wide selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Look for the 🌱 symbol on our menu. We also accommodate special dietary requirements.

Food Safety & Quality

Food safety is our top priority. All our ingredients are fresh, locally sourced when possible, and our kitchen follows strict hygiene standards. We're certified by the local health department.
